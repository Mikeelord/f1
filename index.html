ABOUT US

RWAMUGYENYI HOTEL

We are located in Ntungamu District in the western region of Uganda. Our hotel is located in a good geographic and climatic environment up the mountain that grant a view of sunraise and sunset,a view of Rwashamirwe Town from above, well contracted roads to the hotel, high security, well organised rooms, free wifi, Bar, Sauna, Resturents with all African foods and many other services. visit us for agreat experience

RWAMUGYENYI GRAND MUSEUM

Located right at our Hotel in Ntungamu District. We have collections right from our African ancesters, 1700's, 1800's, 1900's and this includes stones, cars, phones, televisions, newspapers from 1980 to date,and many others

RWAMUGYENYI BUSINESS CENTER

Our Business center is located along Entebbe road just behind Shell Kazi as you branch off to Busabala road. We have rooms suit for Business offices, shops, conference halls, stores, and multi purpose rooms.

RWAMUGYENYI PROJECTS

In the projects our intentions is to promote tourism in Uganda, education, entertainment, talents,the needy like the orphans, and also upholding our socities. In this we have many programs at other centers and departments running those programs I donot think you want to miss up these programs visit the project from the menu for more information
